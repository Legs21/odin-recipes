Peanut Chicken

Description

Peanut Chicken is a highly adaptable recipe based on a thai peanut sauce and a collection of other ingredients. Served over rice, it is a hearty and flavorful dish that is also very easy to make. If you are looking for something that is easy to put together and tastes just as good the next day for lunch, this is absolutely something you should add to your list.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • A Taste of Thai Peanut Sauce Mix
  • Coconut milk
  • Pineapple chunks
  • Peanuts
  • White rice
  • Green beans

Steps

  1. Prep a pot of white rice, amount varies based on how much you wish to make. The rice will cook as you make the rest of the dish.
  2. Cut the chicken breast into pieces and cook it on the stove top. Once fully cooked, remove and set aside for later.
  3. In the same pan we used for the chicken, follow the directions on the "A Taste of Thai Peanut Sauce Mix" to make the sauce.
  4. As the sauce is finishing up, add the green beans and peanuts
  5. Once the sauce is finished, lower the heat and stir in your chicken and pineapple chunks.
  6. Serve over cooked rice for an easy delicious meal.
